    About

    Why did we create this System?

    Getting food from the canteen sometimes means long lines and a lot of walking. Which noticed that students and staff often find it inconvenient. With technology becoming a big part of our lives, we thought about making things easier. That's why we're looking into creating a way to order food online. Imagine being able to pick your favorite meal or snack from your phone or computer, without having to stand in line. This idea came from wanting to save time and make getting food more straightforward for everyone in the school. By looking into this online ordering system, we hope to create a better, simpler way for students and staff to get their meals during school hours.
